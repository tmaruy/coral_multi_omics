=============================================
Multi-omics analysis of coral holobiont
=============================================

Coral holobiont is an ecosystem composed of corals, intracellular algae (zooxanthellae) and resident bacteria.
This study tried to infer their crosstalk by integrative analysis of multi-omics datasets.
